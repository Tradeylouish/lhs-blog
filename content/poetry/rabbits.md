Ancestral lines are often hard to trace  
Forebears doth stretch at times to days of yore  
But rabbit incest of a rapid pace  
Allows us now to honour four  

Progenitors of such a sprawling brood  
All in the Garden of Mt Eden born  
Mayhaps they tasted a forbidden food  
And from on high their sin did draw great scorn  

For now these precious souls have hopped their last  
Cruel verdicts made to seal them a grim fate  
So now is time to fly the flags half-mast  
The rabbits' lease hath all too short a date

---

*Performed live in honour of the lives of the late [Mt Eden rabbits](https://www.stuff.co.nz/environment/300350292/auckland-rabbit-owners-ordered-to-get-rid-of-300-pets-after-neighbours-complain)*
